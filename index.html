Covid-19

Stay Safe, Stay Home!

Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a contagious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first known case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. The disease has since spread worldwide, leading to an ongoing pandemic.

What are the main Symptoms?

Covid-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will be under development of mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

  • Fever
  • Runny Nose
  • Dypsnoea
  • Headache
  • Throat Pain
  • Shaking Chills

Things to do during Covid-19

Things to do to manage your Covid-19 Symptoms :-

  • Wash your Hands for 30 Seconds
  • Wear a Mask
  • Avoid wearing a Filter Mask
  • Regularly change your Disposal Mask if it is possible
  • Keep a Tissue Paper to press the buttons of Elevator
  • Sanitaze your Hands after a public contact

Things not to do during Covid-19

Things not to do to manage your Covid-19 Symptoms :-

  • Do not share Eating
  • Do not touch your Face or Nose
  • Do not contact with sick people
  • Try to avoid going in Public Places if only in Emergency Calls
